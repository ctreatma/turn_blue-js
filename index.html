TurnBlue by Charles Treatman

The goal of this game is to turn the entire board blue. Clicking on a square causes it to change color. But be careful! Adjacent squares will also change color.

On each level, the minimum number of clicks required to turn all squares blue is the same as the level number. If you click too many times in a level, your game is over!
